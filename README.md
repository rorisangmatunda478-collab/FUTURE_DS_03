# FUTURE_DS_03
Task 3- Data science and Analytics
Bank Marketing Strategy & Conversion Analysis

​📊 Project Overview

​This project analyzes a dataset of 45,211 bank marketing leads to identify the drivers behind successful term deposit subscriptions. By leveraging Tableau for visualization and Python for data preparation, I transformed raw campaign data into actionable business intelligence.

​🎯 Key Business Insights

​High-Value Segments: Identified Students (28.68%) and Retired individuals (22.79%) as the highest-converting demographics, outperforming the baseline average of 11.70%.
​The 4-Minute Engagement Rule: Discovered a critical correlation between call duration and success. Unsuccessful calls average 3.7 minutes, whereas successful subscriptions require an average engagement of 8.9 minutes.
​Seasonality: Mapped significant conversion spikes in March, September, and October, providing a roadmap for optimized budget allocation.

​🛠️ Tools & Technologies

​Tableau: Interactive dashboard design, KPI tracking, and trend analysis.
​Python (Pandas/NumPy): Data cleaning, merging 4+ raw files, and feature engineering.
​Data Visualization: Marketing Funnels, Heatmaps, and Time-Series Analysis.

​📈 Dashboard Features

​Executive KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Leads, Successes, and Conversion Rates.
​Marketing Funnel: Visual representation of lead drop-off stages.
​Job & Education Analysis: Segmented performance metrics to guide targeted outreach.
​Duration vs. Success: Behavioral analysis of sales call effectiveness.

​🚀 Recommendations

​Reallocate Budget: Shift 20% of marketing spend from low-performing months (May) to high-performance windows (September).
​Targeted Outreach: Prioritize tertiary-educated leads within the Student and Retired segments.
​Sales Training: Implement a "4-minute hook" training program to increase the probability of moving leads into the high-conversion duration zone.
